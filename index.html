7 Up & Down

Rules are Simple:

  • Two Dice will be rolled together.
  • Numbers on both of the dice will be added.
  • You have to guess that if the number will be greater than 7, Lesser that 7 or Equal to 7.
  • If you think it will be greater than 7 select "UP"
  • If you think it will be smaller than 7 select "DOWN".
  • If you think it will be equal to 7 select "EQUAL"
  • There will be 10 Rounds of 2 points each.
  • If you score greater than Computer then you Win!
